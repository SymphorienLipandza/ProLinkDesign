Revit Architecture

Master building design, structural modeling, and documentation using Revit.

Course Overview

This course is designed for beginners and professionals in architecture and construction who want to master Revit for building design and structural modeling. You will learn how to create detailed architectural and structural models, generate construction documents, and collaborate effectively with teams.

What You Will Learn

  • Building design and modeling using Revit.
  • Structural analysis and documentation.
  • Collaboration tools for team projects.
  • Creating construction documents and schedules.

Requirements

  • Basic computer skills.
  • No prior experience with Revit required.
  • A computer with Revit installed (trial version available).

Course Duration

80 hours of training.
