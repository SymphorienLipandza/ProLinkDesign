V-ray for 3Ds Max

Master photorealistic rendering using V-ray for 3Ds Max.

Course Overview

This course is designed for 3D artists and designers who want to master V-ray for 3Ds Max for creating photorealistic renderings. You will learn how to use V-ray's powerful tools to create stunning visuals for architectural, product, and interior design projects.

What You Will Learn

  • Photorealistic rendering techniques using V-ray.
  • Lighting and shading for realistic scenes.
  • Material creation and texturing.
  • Post-processing and optimization for renderings.

Requirements

  • Basic knowledge of 3Ds Max.
  • No prior experience with V-ray required.
  • A computer with 3Ds Max and V-ray installed (trial versions available).

Course Duration

20 hours of training
